Our Journey

From WordPress Care to Full-Scale WaaS

The Beginning

WordPress Management

Noteracker was born from a simple observation: businesses running websites needed professional, hands-off management not DIY guides and scattered freelancers. We saw too many small businesses struggle with hosting, security, updates, and performance while trying to focus on growth.

Cloud Partnership

Building on Cloud Infrastructure

We partnered with Cloudflare and Vultr the industry's most trusted cloud platforms to provide enterprise-grade hosting and delivery at accessible prices. This infrastructure enables us to deliver 99.9% uptime with global edge performance for businesses of all sizes.

Expansion

Expanding to WaaS

As we managed more client websites, we realized businesses needed more than just hosting: they needed e-commerce, CRM, LMS, and customer support tools all working together. This insight led us to develop our integrated WaaS platform that consolidates everything into one managed service.

Today

Complete WaaS Platform with AI

Our platform now delivers the full spectrum: website design, e-commerce, LMS, CMS, CRM, and our proprietary AI chatbot all managed under one subscription. Every service is built on Cloudflare and Vultr infrastructure, with the same commitment to performance, reliability, and cost efficiency.
