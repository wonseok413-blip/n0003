Common Questions

Plan FAQs

What's included in every plan?

All plans include Cloudflare + Vultr cloud hosting, SSL certificate, daily backups, security monitoring, and CMS management. Higher tiers add e-commerce, CRM, LMS, and AI chatbot services.

How long does the initial setup take?

Starter plans are typically live within 3-5 business days. Standard plans with e-commerce take 5-7 days. Professional plans with LMS and AI chatbot take 7-14 business days depending on complexity.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade anytime — we'll seamlessly add new services to your existing site. The transition is handled by our team with zero downtime.

Do I own my website and data?

Absolutely. You own 100% of your content, designs, and data. If you ever decide to leave, we provide a complete export of your site files and database.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. Plans are month-to-month with the flexibility to cancel anytime. We also offer annual billing with a 20% discount for those who prefer it.
